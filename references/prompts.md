# Example Prompts — CEO Assistant

Use these as-is or adapt them. No special commands needed — just speak naturally.

---

## Finance

- "Explain this P&L to me in plain English"
- "How much runway do we have?"
- "Is our burn rate healthy for our stage?"
- "Why is our gross margin dropping?"
- "Walk me through this cash flow statement"
- "Are we on track to hit our revenue target this quarter?"
- "What does EBITDA mean and why does my investor keep asking about it?"
- "We just got our monthly financials — summarize what I need to know"
- "How do I read a balance sheet?"
- "Our revenue is up but profit is down — what's happening?"
- "Compare our gross margin to industry benchmarks"
- "How much should we be spending on salaries at our stage?"

---

## Sales

- "How is sales tracking this quarter?"
- "Give me a pipeline review"
- "Why are we losing deals?"
- "Our win rate dropped — what should I look at?"
- "Which deals are most at risk right now?"
- "What's a healthy pipeline-to-target ratio?"
- "Our sales cycle is getting longer — what does that usually mean?"
- "Summarize this sales report for me"
- "Which rep needs my attention?"
- "We haven't closed a deal in 3 weeks — what do I do?"
- "How do I calculate CAC from this data?"
- "What's a fair quota for a mid-market AE?"

---

## Marketing

- "How are our marketing channels performing?"
- "Why is our CAC going up?"
- "Is our LTV:CAC ratio healthy?"
- "Which channel is driving the best leads?"
- "Our paid ads spend doubled but leads didn't — what's wrong?"
- "Summarize this marketing report"
- "What's a good email open rate for B2B?"
- "How do I know if our content marketing is working?"
- "We're spending $20K/month on marketing — is that too much for our stage?"
- "Our website traffic is up but signups are flat — why?"
- "What metrics should I ask my marketing team for each week?"

## Customer Success

- "How are our customers doing?"
- "What's our churn rate and is it normal?"
- "Which accounts are at risk?"
- "Our NPS dropped 15 points — what do I do?"
- "What's a healthy churn rate for our stage?"
- "Explain net revenue retention to me"
- "We lost 3 customers this month — what should I look at?"
- "How do I calculate customer lifetime value?"
- "Which customers should I personally call this week?"
- "What does NPS below 20 mean for our business?"

---

## Weekly & Monthly Digest

- "Give me my weekly digest"
- "What's the state of the business this week?"
- "Monthly review — how did we do?"
- "Give me a 5-minute summary of everything"
- "What are the top 3 things I need to focus on this week?"
- "What's the biggest problem in the business right now?"
- "Summarize the week — wins, risks, and priorities"
- "What should I be worried about going into next week?"

---

## HR & Team

- "How is the team doing?"
- "Our attrition went up — what should I investigate?"
- "I need to let someone go — how do I handle it?"
- "How do I give a performance review?"
- "What's a healthy attrition rate for a company our size?"
- "We have 3 open roles that have been open 60+ days — what's wrong?"
- "How do I structure a PIP (performance improvement plan)?"
- "Write a message to the team about our restructure"
- "How do I handle a team member who is underperforming but not enough to fire?"

---

## Hiring

- "Help me hire a VP of Sales"
- "What should I pay a Head of Marketing at our stage?"
- "Write a job description for a Head of Product"
- "What questions should I ask a CFO candidate?"
- "How much equity should I offer a Series A VP?"
- "A candidate countered our offer — how do I negotiate?"
- "What's a good interview process for a senior hire?"
- "How do I structure the first 90 days for a new executive?"
- "What are red flags to watch for when hiring a CRO?"

## Operations & Product

## Investor Relations

## Competitive Intelligence

- "A new competitor just raised $20M — what does that mean for us?"
